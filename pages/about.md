# **About {{ site.author.name }}**

Norwegian Trønder who has spent more than half my life in Sweden — long enough that I express myself more naturally in business Swedish than in business Norwegian. At this point, business English comes more naturally to me than business Norwegian as well.

Since 2010 I have worked full-time with business intelligence and the products in Qlik's Data Analytics platform, primarily [QlikView](https://www.qlik.com/us/products/qlikview) and [Qlik Sense](https://www.qlik.com/us/products/qlik-sense). Over these years I have worked in a wide variety of projects and assignments where business intelligence has been the common denominator.

Today I work at [Etraveli Group](https://www.etraveligroup.com) as a Senior BI Developer, and also serve as acting manager for a Swedish BI team. The BI organization spans teams in Sweden, India, and Greece.

Beyond my day-to-day work, I also engage in the Qlik Community, where I am one of the more active members. In February 2021 my contributions were recognized with a [Round of Applause](https://community.qlik.com/t5/Community-Corner/Round-of-Applause-February-2021/m-p/1781014/highlight/true#M12238), which I found very flattering.
